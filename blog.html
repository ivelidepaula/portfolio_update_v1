5 Podcasts to Listen to If You Are New to Tech


It has not been many months since my podcast addiction came into my life. Since learning a new language (German) has become one of my goals, I have been looking for new tools to facilitate my immersion of the language. That's how I discovered the podcast universe. As I usually spend almost 2 hours a day on public transportation, the podcasts turned out to be my way of learning new things and making my time more productive.

That's why I've separated my 5 favorite podcasts for people, like myself, beginners in the world of technology and programming.

⚬ CodeNewbie

CodeNewbie is the perfect podcast for people learning how to code. It shares the stories of current programmers on their coding journey.



⚬ freeCodeCamp

The famous website/open source community where millions of people learn how to code for free also has its own podcast.



⚬ Learn to Code with me


This podcast is specially aimed for self-taught coders. It is dedicated to help beginners to find useful information that will help them improve their skills.



⚬ Women in Tech


It is not news that women are underrepresented in technology. That's why the host Espree Devora created this podcast to share stories of several women currently working in tech. They tell us how they got to where they currently are and inspire others to achieve their goals as well.



⚬ Revision Path


Revision Path is an amazing podcast that interviews black web devs, web designers and graphic designers throughout the world. The interviewees share their trajectories and perceptions as black people working in the area of technology.
